# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Vibe Trading Desktop is used by finance researchers, quantitative traders, and technically fluent investors who want to move from natural-language strategy ideas to research, backtests, reports, and read-only live runtime checks. They are often working inside a desktop app during active research sessions, comparing assumptions, inspecting risk, and revisiting prior runs.

## Product Purpose

The product gives users a natural-language finance research agent with backtesting, multi-agent workflows, report review, runtime status, and connector-aware analysis. Success means users can start a research task quickly, understand what the agent can and cannot do, inspect evidence, and keep broker-write or live-trading actions clearly bounded by mandate and runtime safeguards.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exacting, and capable. The interface should feel like a serious research desk: confident enough for finance work, quiet enough for long sessions, and explicit when actions touch runtime, mandates, or risk.

## Anti-references

Avoid crypto-casino energy, glossy fintech marketing pages, generic SaaS hero templates, decorative dashboards that bury the task, and dark terminal novelty that makes normal reading harder. Avoid implying that autonomous trading actions are casual or frictionless.

## Design Principles

1. Put the user's next research action in reach.
2. Separate research, reporting, and runtime safety states with clear language and affordances.
3. Prefer dense but scannable layouts over oversized promotional sections.
4. Make data, evidence, and limits visible before visual flourish.
5. Preserve trust through consistent components, readable contrast, and restrained motion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for primary text, controls, and placeholders. Support reduced motion, keyboard focus visibility, dark and light modes, and color choices that do not rely on hue alone for critical status. Keep copy specific for bilingual English and Simplified Chinese users.
